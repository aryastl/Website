ORCAS


If you would like to learn more about orcas click the picture on the right:

They are a type of whale.
It is a toothed whale belonging to the oceanic dolphin family
They are also known as killer whales.
They live with their mothers for their entire life.
They are found in groups because they always stay with each other.
